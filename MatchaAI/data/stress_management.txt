It's easy to get stressed and overwhelmed by the crazy things that occur in our life! Let's work on grounding ourselves by focusing on these steps: 

1. Practice deep breathing until you are able to think clearly 
2. Maintain a consistent sleep schedule to improve your cognitive performance.
3. Engage in regular physical activity like walking, yoga, or cycling.
4. Eat balances meals and avoid excessive caffeine or sugar. Opt for calming but energizing drinks such as matcha and chai. 
5. Use time management tools like planners or digital calendars to avoid becoming overwhelmed.
6. Talk to a friend, teacher, or mental health professional when you need it.

These habits can help reduce stress levels and improve overall mental health.
